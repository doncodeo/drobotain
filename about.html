Shaping the Future, One Innovation at a Time

We are a collective of thinkers, creators, and educators passionate about technology's power to transform Africa.

Our Mission

To build a sustainable drone and robotics industry in Africa by leveraging local talent, fostering STEM skills through hands-on education, and inspiring a new generation of innovators via world-class competitions.

Our Vision

To see an Africa leading the world in drone technology and AI applications, driven by homegrown talent and solutions that solve local challenges and create global opportunities.

Our Core Values

Innovation

We champion creative thinking and the pursuit of groundbreaking solutions.

Education

We believe in the power of knowledge and hands-on skills to unlock potential.

Collaboration

We foster a community where ideas are shared and partnerships thrive.

Integrity

We operate with transparency, ethics, and a commitment to excellence.
