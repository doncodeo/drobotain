Who We Are

Drobotain stands at the forefront of merging education and competition with cutting-edge robotics and drone technology. The goal is to build a sustainable drone industry in Africa, leveraging local talent and resources, and to integrate drone technology into educational curricula.

Areas of Impact

Drobotain drives innovation in STEM education by empowering the next generation with hands-on learning in robotics and drone technology. Through our competitive programs, we foster critical thinking, problem-solving, and creativity while promoting diversity, environmental sustainability, and workforce readiness. By engaging youth, inspiring global collaboration, and advancing technological research, Drobotain creates lasting impact across industries and communities.
